Hello


I'm Quentin, a Program Manager at Apple, living and working in San Francisco. I was born in France and moved to the US in 2019. I graduated from UTC (University of Technology in Compiègne) with a Bachelors in Humanities and Engineering as well as a Masters in Computer Science, in 2017.


Moving to California and living the American dream has been more exciting than anything I could have imagined. The past few years have been full of challenges and learnings, and I have met incredible people from all around the world. I will never thank Apple enough for the opportunity of being part of such an amazing journey. I am deeply grateful for the trust I was given, and thankful to everyone who has supported me along the way.


This website is aimed at introducing myself, as well as topics I care about. Browse safe!
