Welcome.

Hello ! My name is Quentin Keunebroek, I am a 20-year-old French Engineer Student at the University of Technology of Compiègne. This website is aimed at introducing myself as well as presenting the main projects I've been working on over the last few years.


I'm currently participating in the bachelor Humanities and Technology. The aim of this innovative degree course is to train engineers who are able to think globally about the Human-Technologies interactions. Indeed, I'm passionate about everything relating to technologies, and especially about the way that humans and technologies have co-emerged and always co-evolved. That's the reason why I'm getting specialized in the management of socio-technological projects. I'm also deeply interested in animation of labor groups and pedagogy. Besides, I'll study next year in Computer Engineering.


On this website, you can have a look at my projects, check out my resume and get in touch with me ! Do not hesitate to change the language of the website ;)
